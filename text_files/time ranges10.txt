GAS MAIN WORK TO CLOSE PART OF SAINT CLAIR STREET TODAY
 February 28, 2023  News
Saint Clair Street between Second and Sixth Streets in Vincennes will close from 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. today for gas main work.  The City has partnered with CenterPoint Energy to close the road.

That stretch of Saint Clair will be closed to through traffic during those hours.  The closure is expected to last only through the day today.
